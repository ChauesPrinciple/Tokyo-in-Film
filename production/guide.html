Production Stage

This stage is all about putting your pre-production plans into action. You will start filming based on the storyboard, shot list, and script you developed earlier. Whether you are working on location or focusing on choreography, this is where you bring your scenes to life. You will capture raw footage, work with actors, and frame your shots to match your creative vision. Choose one assignment to complete and apply what you learn directly to your own film, developing key production skills like camera work, blocking, and managing the shooting schedule.
