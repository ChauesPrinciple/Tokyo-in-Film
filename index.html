Tokyo in Film

I’m an adventure-driven filmmaker and educator whose work grows out of being on the ground, walking mountain routes, scouting backstreets, filming in real locations, and building maps that connect cinematic storytelling to lived experience.

I created Tokyo in Film and its ongoing location project, documenting shrines, rail lines, neighborhoods, coastlines, and historical sites across Japan. My videography ranges from action choreography with local stunt teams to travel films and cultural fieldwork. I’ve carried this approach across more than thirty countries over three passports, letting each place shape the way I frame what I see.

If you would like to hire me for professional planning or as an on-site guide, I can provide travel itineraries for places you may not otherwise find and/or full logistical planning. If you would like to hire me for videography or editing, please contact me below.
