Action Units

2–4 Action Unit Worksheet

This worksheet captures one Hong Kong–style action unit, defined as 2–4 continuous physical actions performed and filmed in order, without pause or reset.

This follows the method Quentin Tarantino describes when breaking down action scenes such as Kill Bill Vol. 1:

  • Action is broken into small groups of physical movements
  • Each group is performed and shot as written
  • The camera follows the action
  • Continuity is created by what happens on camera, not fixed later

This sheet records only what happens next. It does not plan the entire fight.
