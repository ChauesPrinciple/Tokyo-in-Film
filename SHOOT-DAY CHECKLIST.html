SHOOT-DAY CHECKLIST

BEFORE YOU LEAVE HOME

☐ Storyboard printed or saved
☐ Shot list prepared with priorities
☐ Location-scout notes reviewed
☐ Reference images and test frames ready on phone
☐ Camera, lenses, filters packed
☐ Tripod or stabilizer packed
☐ Props, wardrobe, continuity items packed
☐ Batteries fully charged with spares
☐ Media cards cleared and formatted
☐ Audio gear packed
☐ Call sheet and schedule reviewed

ON LOCATION — BEFORE SETUP

☐Confirm access to the space matches the scout
☐Confirm light conditions match what was planned
☐Check sun position or interior light direction
☐Confirm all actors or participants have arrived
☐Check ambient noise against scout notes
☐Walk the space and verify storyboard angles still work
☐Identify hazards, unexpected obstacles, or weather issues
☐Make any immediate adjustments to framing or movement

CAMERA SETUP

☐Place camera in the planned angle
☐Match focal length to storyboard
☐Confirm distance and height from scout notes
☐Lock tripod or stabilizer
☐Set exposure
☐Set focus
☐Set white balance
☐Check for flares, reflections, or moving shadows
☐Confirm camera movement path is clear

LIGHTING SETUP

☐Review natural or existing light sources
☐Position subject to match planned key direction
☐Add or remove fill as needed
☐Check and control shadows
☐Eliminate unwanted reflections
☐Maintain lighting consistency across shots
☐Confirm skin tone and wardrobe detail look correct under real light

SOUND SETUP

☐Record room tone
☐Check for wind or environmental noise
☐Check mic placement and distance
☐Confirm levels are not clipping
☐Identify intermittent or unpredictable noises
☐Note any issues that may require ADR or foley later

BLOCKING

☐Place actors according to storyboard positions
☐Rehearse movement with camera
☐Ensure movement reads clearly in frame
☐Confirm eyelines and motion direction
☐Adjust pacing or marks for the real location
☐Match background and foreground interaction to storyboard intent

SHOOTING

☐Confirm framing matches storyboard
☐Confirm actor placement and timing
☐Confirm lighting consistency
☐Record a clean take
☐Check playback for focus, continuity, exposure, shadows, background issues
☐Record a second take as safety
☐Mark the strongest take
☐Move on only when the shot is correct

BEFORE LEAVING THE LOCATION

☐Review every shot in order
☐Confirm emotional beat and clarity
☐Confirm beginning–middle–end flow
☐Confirm transitions between shots are covered
☐Capture quick pickups if needed
☐Check audio quality again
☐Make sure you will not need the location again
☐Sweep for all gear and props

BACKUP IMMEDIATELY AFTER

☐Copy all footage to at least one drive
☐Confirm files open and play correctly
☐Organize footage into labeled folders
☐Write notes for editing about continuity, color, audio, or pacing fixes
