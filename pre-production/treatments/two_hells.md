## Why This Works
The original Inferno/Se7en pairing told you that contrapasso mirrors without teaching. This shows two mirroring systems: one that cannot teach (Sibyl executes, continues running), one that learns (Decim weeps, changes). The viewer is not told which is Dante's answer. The final blinking text—unstable, unresolvable—forces them to complete the circuit themselves.

The hook is the split frame. The experience is the two completions. The CTA is the clash that doesn't resolve.
